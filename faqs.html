Frequently asked questions

• Where can I buy a tour?

You can choose a resort and hotel by yourself on favsuitcase.com, and book your tour online. You can also call us at 8(800) 387-75-12 or visit a Favourite Suitcase agency and our manager will choose the tour for you.

• Do you do discounts on tours?

When you buy a tour online on the site or in our own travel agencies, we give you a discount in accordance with current promotions. Please note that discounts do not add up.

• How many days in advance can I book my tour?

- to visa-free countries - from 3 days before departure;

- Visa countries: 9 to 14 days before departure.

• What is a "last minute ticket"?

It is a special offer from tour operator for specific dates and hotels. Tour operator buys seats on the plane, hotel rooms, paid transfers, etc. for specific dates (or for the whole season). But if some tours remain unsold, the competition between tour operators begins to rapidly reduce prices, which is very profitable for the client.

• How to get a visa for traveling?

The main condition of getting entry visa is to submit in advance a full package of documents, collected in strict accordance with the requirements of a particular consulate (passport, application form, photo, etc.).
